About

Helper is a connection service that sends an alert to all volunteers in the area and let's them know they have opportunity to be part of your organizations. The volunteers recieve a text about opportunites and are able to respond whether they can attend. The organization then can see volunteers signing up to be part of the experience and know how many to expect.
